78.4 Environment operations

Function: getenv (env) ¶

Get the value of the environment variable env

Example: 

(%i1) load("operatingsystem")$
(%i2) getenv("PATH");
(%o2) /usr/local/sbin:/usr/local/bin:/usr/sbin:/usr/bin:/sbin:/bin
